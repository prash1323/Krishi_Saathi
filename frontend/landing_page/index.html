Our Purpose

Why Krishi Saathi exists

India's 100 million+ farming families face daily challenges that no one technology has solved holistically. Crop failures, lack of market intelligence, and inaccessible government support cost farmers billions every year.

Krishi Saathi bridges the gap between cutting-edge AI and the hands that feed a nation — delivering actionable intelligence directly to farmers in their language, on their device.
