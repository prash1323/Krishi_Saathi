# 🌾 Krishi Saathi
### Empowering Agriculture with Smart Technology

Krishi Saathi is a smart agricultural assistance platform built to help farmers make data-driven decisions by integrating modern technology into farming practices. The platform aims to solve common agricultural challenges such as crop selection, disease detection, weather uncertainty, and market price awareness through a centralized digital solution.

The project is designed with scalability, accessibility, and practical utility in mind to support farmers in improving productivity and minimizing losses.

---

## 📖 Problem Statement

Agriculture remains the backbone of many economies, yet farmers continue to face several challenges:

- Lack of proper crop planning
- Unpredictable weather conditions
- Late detection of plant diseases
- Limited access to market price trends
- Lack of awareness about government schemes
- Inefficient resource management

These challenges often result in lower productivity, financial losses, and poor decision-making.

**Krishi Saathi** addresses these issues by creating an intelligent support system for farmers.

---

## 🎯 Vision

To create a digital ecosystem that makes farming smarter, more efficient, and more accessible by providing farmers with timely insights and practical solutions.

---

## 🚀 Objectives

### 🌱 Crop Identification
Provide detailed information about crops including:
- Crop type
- Growth cycle
- Water requirements
- Fertilizer requirements
- Suitable seasons

---

### 🦠 Disease Detection
Allow farmers to identify crop diseases at an early stage through image-based analysis and receive treatment recommendations.

Key benefits:
- Early prevention
- Reduced crop damage
- Better yield quality

---

### ☁️ Weather Forecasting
Provide real-time weather insights such as:
- Temperature
- Humidity
- Rainfall probability
- Wind speed
- Seasonal patterns

This helps farmers plan irrigation, sowing, and harvesting.

---

### 🌾 Crop Recommendation System
Suggest the most suitable crops based on:
- Soil type
- Region
- Weather conditions
- Water availability
- Season

---

### 📈 Market Price Tracking
Help farmers monitor current crop prices in markets to make better selling decisions.

Features:
- Crop-wise market rates
- Price trends
- Profitability analysis

---

### 🏛 Government Scheme Portal
Provide awareness about:
- Agricultural subsidies
- Crop insurance
- Loan assistance
- Government welfare schemes

---

### 💡 Smart Farming Assistance
Guide farmers on:
- Fertilizer usage
- Pest management
- Irrigation optimization
- Sustainable farming methods
